Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

KonGuard ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered antivirus application for Android. We take your privacy seriously.

Data We Collect

  • Email address – for account registration and login (Firebase Authentication)
  • Scan logs – file names and URLs scanned, results (stored in Firebase Firestore)
  • Payment info – sender name and payment confirmation (no card numbers stored)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, messages, or personal files
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not store card numbers or financial credentials

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication and database
  • VirusTotal – file and URL scanning
  • Google Gemini AI – threat analysis

Accessibility Service

KonGuard uses Android Accessibility Service to monitor URLs in messenger apps for malware detection. This data is processed locally and only suspicious URLs are sent to scanning services. We do not read your personal messages.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us.
